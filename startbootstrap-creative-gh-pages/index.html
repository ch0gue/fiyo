About Us

FIYO Construction is a team of highly skilled designers, building code consultants, electricians and project managers with years of field experience and knowledge to deliver incredible results: quickly and seamlessly. We provide a host of services in home renovations – all the way from design to construction. Whether it’s a new rec room or a separate livable unit, we have the expertise to get you there.

Our team leverages their experience in the Ontario Building Code and Electrical Code to make sure your vision comes to life in an affordable and safe way.

Our Services

Basement Renovations
Need more space for your growing family? Have an unfinished basement? FIYO construction can customize your home to meet your needs.
Secondary Dwelling Units
Tired of the increasing prices of detached homes? Have extra space that you want to rent out? FIYO Construction can convert your home into a legal secondary unit/ or duplex.
Kitchen Remodeling
Looking to re-model your kitchen? Tired of your out-dated kitchen? FIYO Construction can design and build the kitchen of your dreams within your budget.
Bathroom Renovation
Dislike the design of your existing bathroom? Looking for a his and her sink? Want to remove the traditional tub and have a modern shower? FIYO Construction can be your one stop shop from the design stages to the final stages.
Take Over Projects
Construction partially completed at your home and it doesn’t look like what you had hoped for? FIYO Construction can step in and make the transition and complete the job stress free.
Customization Concepts
Do you have some custom ideas about your home but can’t turn those dreams into reality? FIYO Constructions highly experienced design and construction team can work within your budget and turn that dream into a reality.
